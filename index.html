  • Bye - baɪ

    The position of a person or team in a tournament or competition who draws no opponent in a particular
    round so advances to the next round unopposed, or is awarded points for a win in a league table; also the phantom opponent of such a person or team.

    Example: “Craig's Crew plays the bye next week.”

    "An extra scored when the batsmen take runs after the ball has passed the striker without hitting either the bat or the batsman."
